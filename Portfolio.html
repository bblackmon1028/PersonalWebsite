Fitness Center

Fitness Center features a Console App for Grand Circus gym. Application users can add/remove Members, check Into a gym and view member information. It also features a text file used to store all membership information. Check it all out here: Fitness Center!

Roshambo

Roshambo is a Console App for that allows you to play Rock, Paper, Scissors. Start by choosing your opponent: Random or Rock player. Then select your choice. The players face off and the winner is announced! Play the game here: Roshambo!
